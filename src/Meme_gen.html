About Meme Gen

This is a little project of mine where I was interested into learning a little bit of a new coding language. Here I chose python in combination with Flask, with Flask I could set up a a small web application. It generates a new meme every 10 seconds. The memes are coming from a subreddit, however I do have the choice to turn NSFW on, but for social reasons I keep it Safe for work friendly :D.
