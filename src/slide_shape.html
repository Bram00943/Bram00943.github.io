About Image Reveal & Shape Sorter

These were 2 projects I've worked on and completed during my internship at @Livewall. Image Reveal is in the video on the left and Shape sorter is in the video on the right. Image reveal it's purpose is eg when someone or a company wants to reveal their new logo they can use this application which is offered by Livewall to reveal it. Other things can be done aswell with the Image Reveal, say you want to suprise someone with a picture, you can use the Image Reveal! The Shape Sorter is a little progress game where you have to drag the right figure to the corresponding hole. If the figure fits in the hole then you'll gain a point on the Image Reveal and so forth. If the figure doesn't fit in the hole it snaps back to it's original position, and you'll gain no points.

My contribution

With the Image Reveal I was done fairly quickly since it wasn't a too big of a feature but since I didn't have a lot of knowledge with JS React it took maybe a little longer then it should have if I did have the experience. The shape sorter on the other hand was a difficult one, since I needed to start from scratch again because all I got was the basic preset of the application from Livewall with nothing in it or on it. So I broke the feature up into little pieces and piece by piece I started developing and testing.
